Social Media Do’s

•	Think carefully before posting anything online. 
•	Maintain a respectful and professional tone. 
•	Protect your personal information and privacy. 
•	Share useful educational or professional content. 
•	Verify information before sharing it. 


Social Media Don’ts

•	Do not post offensive or inappropriate content. 
•	Avoid spreading fake news or misinformation. 
•	Do not share confidential or personal details publicly. 
•	Avoid engaging in online arguments or harassment. 
•	Do not use someone else's content without permission.
