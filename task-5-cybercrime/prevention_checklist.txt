Stay Safe Online

1.	Never approve UPI collect requests unless you are sure about the sender. 
2.	Remember: Scanning a QR code is for paying money, not receiving it. 
3.	Do not share OTP, UPI PIN, bank details, or passwords with anyone. 
4.	Verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks or payment apps. 
5.	Avoid clicking suspicious links received through SMS, email, or WhatsApp. 
6.	Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts. 
7.	Use strong and unique passwords for banking and payment apps. 
8.	Install apps only from trusted sources like official app stores. 
9.	Always check website URLs before entering payment information. 
10.	Immediately report fraud to National Cyber Crime Portal – https://cybercrime.gov.in or call 
Helpline 1930.
